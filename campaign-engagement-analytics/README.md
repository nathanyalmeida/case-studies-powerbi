## Overview

This Power BI dashboard monitors supporter engagement activity across campaigns, providing a consolidated view of interaction metrics, participation trends, and demographic distribution.

The report focuses on tracking engagement behavior over time and understanding how supporters interact with campaign initiatives.

## Analytical Focus

The dashboard enables analysis of:

* Total supporter base and engagement activity
* Interaction trends over time
* Participation distribution by demographic characteristics
* Engagement levels across campaign topics or initiatives

The objective is to support campaign monitoring and provide visibility into supporter activity dynamics.

## Dashboard Structure

### Engagement Overview

Presents key engagement indicators such as:

* Total supporters
* Interaction volume
* Participation metrics
* Campaign activity signals

### Engagement Trends

Time-series visualization of supporter activity to monitor evolution of engagement over time.

### Demographic Distribution

Breakdown of engagement by:

* Gender
* Age group

### Campaign Interaction

Analysis of supporter participation by campaign topic or action type.

## Analytical Approach

* KPI-driven engagement monitoring
* Behavioral activity tracking
* Demographic segmentation visualization
* Campaign performance observation
