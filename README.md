## Case Studies

### Brazilian E-commerce Customer Behavior

Analysis of marketplace transactions using the Olist public dataset to evaluate customer purchasing patterns, logistics performance, and revenue distribution across product categories.

### Sales Year-over-Year Performance Analysis

Comparative analysis of departmental sales dynamics across two years, identifying revenue trends, customer segment shifts, and seasonal purchasing behaviors to support strategic planning.

### Campaign Engagement Analytics

Evaluation of supporter interaction across advocacy campaigns, integrating email engagement, petition participation, donation patterns, and demographic insights to inform engagement strategies.

### Municipal Data Publication Analysis

Assessment of digital publication practices among Brazilian municipalities with populations above 100k inhabitants, analyzing data transparency maturity through publication formats, domains, and availability timelines.
