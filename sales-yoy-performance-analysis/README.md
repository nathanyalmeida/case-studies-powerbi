## Overview

This Power BI report presents a structured analytical narrative of sales performance across departments and customer segments over a two-year period.

The report is designed in a presentation-style layout to communicate key business findings, highlight performance shifts, and support strategic decision-making discussions.

## Analytical Focus

The analysis explores:

* Year-over-year sales variation by department
* Changes in customer segment performance
* Identification of growth and decline patterns
* Key commercial highlights and performance drivers

The objective is to transform comparative sales data into clear insights that can inform marketing and commercial strategy.

## Report Structure

### Introduction

Provides business context and explains the analytical scope, focusing on identifying meaningful trends in consumer behavior and departmental performance.

### Key Sales Highlights

Summarizes main performance outcomes through:

* Departmental sales comparison (current vs previous year)
* YoY growth indicators
* Trend direction signals
* Executive summary of main observations

### Strategic Insights

Translates analytical findings into suggested strategic directions, including:

* Opportunities in high-growth departments
* Attention areas in declining segments
* Customer retention considerations
* Commercial prioritization guidance

## Analytical Approach

* Comparative performance analysis
* Business storytelling through structured BI layout
* Executive-oriented KPI communication
* Insight-driven narrative design
